## What you will learn

- How to set up a Node.
- How to create your first Service and expose it to the internet.

## Prerequisities

- You have to be registered on ptah.sh.
- Your account must be fully activated (E-Mail verified & subscription started).
- You have a created VPS with Ubuntu on board on the provider of your choise. We recommend [Hetzner](https://dub.sh/ptah-sh-hetzner) due to their low price tag and yet exceptional performance.

## What you will get

- Running Node (server).
- Exposed nginx service to the internet.
